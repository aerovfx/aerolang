	•	The flower that symbolizes Tet in southern Vietnam is the apricot blossom.

10. Fit / Suitable / Becoming
	•	Fit (v): vừa vặn
	•	Suitable (adj) for: phù hợp
	•	Becoming to (adj): hợp, đẹp với ai đó

Ví dụ:
	•	Your shirt fits your body.
	•	Your shirt is fit for your body.
	•	Peach blossom is not fit for hot weather.
